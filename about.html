Our Mission

The Berry College trail network connects students, staff, and visitors with nature, providing opportunities for recreation, education, and ecological research.

History

Berry College has been maintaining and developing its trail system for decades. Trails such as the Viking Trail and House o’ Dreams Trail reflect both the college’s natural beauty and historical landmarks.

Conservation & Research

Many trails pass through conservation areas and restoration projects, including the Longleaf Pine restoration project. Students and faculty use the trails as outdoor classrooms to study ecology, wildlife, and environmental management.
